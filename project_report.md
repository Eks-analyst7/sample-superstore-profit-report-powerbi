# Sample Superstore Profit Report using Power BI

## Project Overview

This project is an interactive Business Intelligence dashboard built in Power BI using the Sample Superstore dataset.

The dashboard analyzes overall business performance by exploring sales, profit, quantity sold, customer segments, geographical regions, and product categories.

The objective is to transform raw transactional data into meaningful business insights that support data-driven decision making.

## Business Questions Answered

- Which regions generate the highest profit?
- Which customer segments are the most profitable?
- Which product categories perform best?
- How has profit changed over time?
- Which sub-categories contribute the most profit?

---

## Key Insights

- The West region contributes the highest profit.
- Consumer customers generate the largest share of profit.
- Technology is the top-performing category.
- Profit has grown over multiple years with some fluctuations.
- Copiers, Phones, and Accessories are among the most profitable sub-categories.
